The best weather conditions for rice cultivation are12345:

Warm, tropical climates with temperatures between 70°F and 90°F.
High humidity levels.
Abundant water supply.
Prolonged sunshine.
Yearly rainfall between 69 and 118 inches (175 and 300 cm) on average.
Paddies inundated for at least 10 weeks during the growing season.
Water depths ranging from 1 inch (25 mm) at the transplanting stage to as much as 6 inches (150 mm) throughout the crop lifecycle.
